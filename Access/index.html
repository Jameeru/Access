About Us

We strive to make our content accessible to everyone.

  • Text-to-Speech: Our website supports text-to-speech functionality for those who prefer auditory content consumption.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements can be accessed and operated using keyboard navigation, ensuring that users who cannot use a mouse can fully interact with our site.
  • Color Contrast: We use high-contrast colors to make text readable for users with visual impairments.
  • Alternative Text: All images on our site include descriptive alternative text for users who rely on screen readers.
  • Resizable Text: Text on our site can be resized without loss of content or functionality, allowing users to adjust text size according to their needs.
